Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all Bonsai… apps, including WordPath, SoundBloom, and Tutti Fruttini. By using these apps, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information directly. Our apps:

  • Do not require registration or login
  • Do not collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Do not access your contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera
  • Do not track your precise location

Some apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address (approximate location such as city/country)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • Technical data such as crash reports and performance metrics

This data is collected and processed by Google according to their policies: Google Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

Bonsai… apps store only local game or app data on your device, such as settings, preferences, or high scores. We do not upload this data to any server.

Google AdMob may use its collected data to:

  • Provide personalized or non‑personalized ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

User Choices

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Or: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete Advertising ID

Some apps (like Tutti Fruttini) only show ads when you choose to watch a rewarded ad. You may use the app without ever watching ads.

Data Storage

All app data (such as high scores or settings) is stored locally on your device using the app’s internal storage. No cloud sync or external storage is used.

Children’s Privacy

Bonsai… apps do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. AdMob is configured to serve age‑appropriate ads. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.
